Snake fight

Snake fight was the first multiplayer game created by Finn.
This game is not to different from tron, if you've heard of the game.

The game is simple. Aim your snake with the appropriate keys, if you hit a wall or a snake's body, you lose.

Each game can vary greatly, not to mentin the diferent gamemodes you can play.
There can be up to four people playing at once, however it gets quite cramped on the keyboard, when you go past two players.

The diferent gamemodes you can play are:

  • Normal: Nothing special about this.
  • Pellets: Eating diferent pellets will give you diferent effects.
  • Reversed: All your controls are reversed.
  • Maze: Play inside of a maze.
