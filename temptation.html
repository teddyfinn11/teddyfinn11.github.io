Temptation

Temptation was a game started in Finn's free time, however it soon became his newest assignment.
The game is centered around the idea of temptation. infront of you sits a big red button. What does it do? Should I push it?
no one told me not too.

There are multiple endings to this game, as marked out by polaroids stuck to your wall when you complete one.
The easiest ending to get is the the assasin ending.
To get this ending, you push the button and then flip the new switch before pushing the button again.

There are three other endings out there, but I'll leave them for you to discover. If you need any help, you can consult the discord.
Just know there are more tools then there first seems.
