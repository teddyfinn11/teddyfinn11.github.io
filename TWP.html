Thinking with puzzles

Thinking with puzzles was a more challenging game to make then 1945, the previous game in the series.
It required that Finn changed from the two dimentional view gamemaker was desinded for, to a three dimentional view he built himself. Not only that, but he would have to make every sprite himself, because of his own morals.
This lead him to create over 70 hand made sprites, pixel by pixel using nothing but piskel.

The game starts you on a bed in a house. From here you must navigate outdoors and follow the path to the lake.
Once you reach the lake, you'll see a guy behind you before the screen goes black, and you wake up in a dungen.
From here you have to solve a series of puzzles, before fighting the 'puzzle master' and escaping.
