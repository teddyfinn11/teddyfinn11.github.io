How it started.

Nova games began when Finn Mortensen, the creater of Nova games, got an assignment from his teacher Miss Scott.

The assignment was to create a game, where the player controlled a plane and had to destroy enemy planes to get points.

Finn created the game and promptly named it 1945. However, thinking it best the game doesn't throw you straight into a fight.
He put a fake company on the start up menu as a joke. And thus, Nova games was born.

The next steps.

From here, Finn was assigned more games to make and next up on the plater was Thinking with puzzles.

Thinking with puzzles, or TWP, was an ambitious game.
This game brought many challenges to Finn who was still getting use to gamemaker. One such challenge was changing from the standard 2 dimensional view gamemaker was designed for, to the 3 dimensional view he intended.

Another challenge Finn faced, was his intention to make every Sprite himself. This concluded with over 70 hand made Sprites, using nothing but piskel.

Over time, Finn has made more free to play games, snake fight, temptation, ect...
I hope you enjoy all the games He's released, and make sure to check the discord for any new updates.
